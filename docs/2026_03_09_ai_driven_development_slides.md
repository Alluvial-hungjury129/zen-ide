Slide 1: Title Slide

Title: AI-Driven Development: Integrating Intelligence and Engineering Excellence

Subtitle: A Roadmap for Modern Software Craftsmanship

Slide 2: Defining AI-Driven Development

Title: Beyond Automation: A Dynamic System

    AI-driven development requires a flexible system of governance.1
    The decision framework, such as an AGENTS.md file, must be dynamic.1
    Continuously keep increasing decisions, guidelines, DOs, and DON'Ts.1

Slide 3: The Pitfall of "Vibe Coding"

Title: Why Structure Matters More Than Ever

    One major problem in modern development is "vibe coding".1
    This unstructured approach leads to unpredictable outcomes and technical debt.1
    The goal of AI-driven development is to create a guided, predictable process, not simply automate poor habits.1

Slide 4: Caution: Avoiding Rigid Specifications

Title: The AI Development Paradox

    Traditional development methods built around rigid, pre-defined specs (often referred to as "waterfall") are problematic when working with AI.1
    AI development benefits from agility and the ability to adapt as systems learn and evolve.1
    Rigid specification approaches can slow down iteration and innovation when integrating AI tools.1

Slide 5: Maximizing Results from LLMs

Title: Treat Your LLM Like a Software Engineer

    The absolute best results from Large Language Models (LLMs) come from treating them as a software engineer.1
    Focus on applying Normal Dev Things.1
    LLM-specific prompt engineering or techniques are transient and will likely be gone soon.1

Slide 6: The Core Engineering Disciplines

Title: Essential Practices for AI Workflows

    Successfully implementing AI-driven development relies on foundational engineering skills:1
        Scope the ticket properly.1
        Break the work down.1
        Test well (includes Automatic Testing).1
        Write the correct documentation.1

Slide 7: The Value of Human Craftsmanship

Title: Empowering Manual Work and Creativity

    The pleasure and value in programming come from continually improving the craft.1
    This practice involves learning the underlying truths about computation and applying what you've learned.1
    The enjoyment of programming is intrinsic and remains regardless of whether an AI assists in coding.1

Slide 8: The Human Role in Improvement

Title: Deepening Understanding: Beyond the Code

    Human curiosity is the driving force that always brings a programmer back to the work.1
    A key skill is wanting to know how things work, how to make the code better, and what is holding the AI itself back from being a more effective programmer.1
    Human oversight ensures the AI is directed towards better, more efficient solutions.1

Slide 9: Enduring Motivation

Title: Taking Apart the Watch to See How it Ticks

    The desire to understand the mechanics persists through cycles of hype and collapse.1
    Even if many jobs disappear, the human impulse remains to understand low-level details (e.g., how a Linux kernel driver works).1
    This deep understanding is the unique contribution of the human developer, ensuring quality and innovation in the AI-driven system.1

Slide 10: Key Takeaways

Title: AI-Driven Development: A Hybrid Approach

    System Governance: Implement a dynamic and evolving set of guidelines for AI usage.1
    Foundational Skills: Rely on core engineering discipline (scoping, testing, documentation) over short-lived AI tricks.1
    The Human Factor: Manual expertise and intellectual curiosity are essential for continuous improvement and maximizing AI effectiveness.1
    The Craft: The lasting value and enjoyment of programming come from the relentless pursuit of learning and mastering the underlying truths of computation.1
